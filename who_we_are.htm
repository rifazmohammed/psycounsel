Who We Are

PsyCounsel is co-ordinated by Ms Loney Jacob. Loney Jacob is a Gold medalist in Psychology from the University of Delhi. She has done further courses in Counseling Psychology from the University of Akron, Ohio, US. She has visited the erstwhile USSR, USA and West Indies during her student days. Besides a stint as a lecturer for PG students at the SNDT University, Mumbai, Ms Jacob has worked as a Consultant Clinical Psychologist at the Spastic Society of India, Mumbai, and the Naval Hospital at Visakhapatnam in the past. She presently works as a Clinical Psychologist at the Peejay's Child Guidance Clinic at Kochi.


Ms Jacob is a member of many professional bodies like the International Society of Clinical Psychologists, Indian Association of Clinical Psychologists, Indian Association for Child and Adolescent Mental Health. She is also a registered professional with the Rehabilitation Council of India.She takes keen interest in areas like NLP and Emotional Intelligence. She co-ordinates these resources to optimal effect in the practice of Psychology and maintains close contact with experts in these fields.

He has conducted several programmes for schools, institutions of higher education, techno-commercial organisations, and social groups. Workshops by Dr Abraham can be organized through PsyCounsel. PsyCounsel utilises Dr Abraham's inputs and expertise to the benefit of it's clients.
